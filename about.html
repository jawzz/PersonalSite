Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Josh Fox, an Intelligent Automation Program Advisor. I'm passionate about making systems more efficient, exploring the cosmos, and practicing mindfulness. My journey has taken me from the cutting edge of automation technologies to the calm of meditation and the mysteries of space.

My Philosophy
